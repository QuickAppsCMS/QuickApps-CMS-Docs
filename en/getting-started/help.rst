Where to Get Help
#################

The Official QuickAppsCMS website
----------------------------------

http://www.quickappscms.org/

It features links to downloads and important announcements such as new releases and
important bug fixes.

The Google Group
----------------

http://groups.google.com/group/quickapps-cms

It can be a great resource for finding archived answers, frequently asked questions,
and getting answers to immediate problems.
